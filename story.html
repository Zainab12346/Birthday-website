Where It All Began

Every beautiful love story has a beginning, and ours started in the most unexpected way.

We met at New York Car Park by the beach. I was walking home alone while you were watching me from a distance. Later, you told me that you couldn't stop looking at me.

As I continued walking, you slowly drove your car behind me and asked, "Where are you heading?"

I ignored you and kept walking.

You came back again... and again... asking the same question. I kept ignoring you because I didn't know you.

Finally, I stopped and asked, "What do you want? Why do you keep following me?"

You smiled and simply said, "Let me give you a ride."

Neither of us knew that this small conversation would become the beginning of something so beautiful.

Looking back today, I'm grateful that you didn't give up that day, because meeting you changed my life in the most wonderful way.
